“C” DESIGNATES CONFERENCE MEETING

“B” DESIGNATES BOARD MEETING

Combined Conference/Board Meetings are herein designated as Type Meeting “C/B”, at which formal action shall be taken.

All combined Conference/Board Meetings, unless otherwise indicated, will be will be held in the Essex County Board of County Commissioners Board Room Chambers, 5th Floor, Room 506, Hall of Records, Newark, New Jersey.

Meetings will be available to view at www.ecfnj.com; and can also be viewed on the Essex County Commissioners Facebook page and YouTube channel.
